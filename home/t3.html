New COVID-19 vaccine approved in the UK

The government has approved the use of the new Moderna vaccine in the UK. 17 million doses have already been pre ordered and will arrive during spring.
This is the last Covid Vaccine following the approval of the previous vaccines Pfizer and AstraZeneca. More are still in development.
It has been recordered that at least 1.5 million people in the UK have already had at least one dose of the vaccine.
The most vulnerable people are being vaccinated first. The aim of the Prime minister is to vaccinate as mush as 15 million people by February.
Adding the Moderna vaccines to the already pre ordered vaccines, this raises the total vaccines pro ordered to a combined total of 367 million doses.
The Moderna vaccine is an RNA vaccine similar to the Pfizer vaccine and has to be kept in storage of -20C unlike the Pfizer which has to be kept in tempearatures of around -70C.
Between the 3 vaccines the Astrezenaca vaccine is much easier to store as it can be kept in storage of regular fridge temperature.
The Moderna vaccine has been said to deliver nearly 95% protection from severe COVID.
