UK has highest daily death rate in the world



The UK recently broke its record for the most average deaths since the pandemic started Now it has the world's worst daily death rate for Coronavirus. On average 935 people die daily after testing positive.

Data collected by an Oxford University research platform indicated that the UK has the worst weej average of coronavirus deaths. The rate per million people is 16.54.

The United Kingdom surpassed Czech Republic who had highest rate since early January after publishing its new data just Sunday. Czech republic have a death rate of 16.32.

Portugal come third at a rate of 14.82, followed by Slovakia who have a rate of 14.55 then Lithuania at 13.01.

However the Northern Ireland Secretary stated on ITV's Good Morning Britain that its "too early" for comparisons.

He further continued by stating international comparisons on death tolls are not appropriate till after the pandemic.

The total UK death toll is now at 89,860 rising by 599 on Monday. This is the highest in Europe. Only US, Brazil, Mexico and indicated have higher death tolls.
